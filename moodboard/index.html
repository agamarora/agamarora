SVG cursive / animated

Notes on the mark

  • Lowercase aa. Not AA. Not Aa. Lowercase is personal, not corporate.
  • Cursive/handwritten for loading animation. Feels like a signature being drawn.
  • Glow variant for loading states. Pulsing gold on dark. The site breathing.
  • Static variant for favicon, nav corner. Needs to work at 16px.

07 — Decisions

What this site IS

  • Personality-first. Not a portfolio. Not a resume. You, your takes, your energy.
  • Opinion-forward. Your AI takes are the content that converts.
  • Proof through craft. The site itself proves you have taste and can build.
  • Extensible. Phase 1 is the home page. Lab/Explore come later.
  • Dark. This is your native environment.

What this site IS NOT

  • A portfolio (not enough projects yet, and that's fine)
  • A resume (the resume isn't working, so why copy it)
  • An editorial magazine (too impersonal)
  • A SaaS landing page (you're not a product)
  • Someone else's design (not Edwin, not Linear, not anyone)
