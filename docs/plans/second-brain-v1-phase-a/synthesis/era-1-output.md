Yet 2016-01-02 (urn:6089072870409908224) framed V2 Games itself using "The Chakravyuh" — a military-conquest metaphor. Shift: from conquest-metaphor founder in 2016 to more reflective market-share-thinker by late 2017. Note: a 20-month maturation across the early-founder → maturing-founder arc.

- **LinkedIn as platform:** Early E1 posts (2014-2015) are zero-effort one-liners with no strategic intent (5922288282724487168, 5925108053526466560). By 2017-01 (6228231934921797632), Agam is explicitly experimenting with "LinkedIn's broken algorithm" for distribution. Evolution: passive-user → active-platform-gamer inside three years.

- **Hiring tone evolution:** 2016-01-02 (6089072870409908224) hiring post is maximalist/poetic ("frontline entrepreneurs", "Chakravyuh"). 2016-03-17 (6116167384093970432) hiring post is boilerplate JD-style. 2017-06-30 (6286457096145825792) is one-paragraph-and-out. The voice de-escalates from *movement* to *logistics* as the company matures — an interesting arc but possibly also fatigue.

## 7. Era boundary notes

**Proposed refinement:** E1 should be re-cut as **2014-2015 (Absolutdata / student → analyst phase)** and **2016-2017 (V2 Games founder phase)** — these are two genuinely different Agams, and collapsing them hides structure.

Evidence:
- 2014-2015 posts (9 total) are almost all <20-word one-liners reacting to external links. Voice: *observer*. Low effort, few personal claims. Sits on top of student+analyst-1st-job identity.
- 2016-01 onward, posts acquire *announcement* and *recruiting* shape (eSports manifesto, hiring calls for engineers/analysts). Voice: *operator / founder*. A sharp phase change.
- The 2017-12 retrospective + 2017-12-21 ML-platforms hot-take + 2017-09-14 dominate-vs-compete reflection form a *proto-reflective* voice at the very end of E1 that foreshadows E2. That arc suggests **2017 end-of-year posts could belong more naturally with early E2** (2018-2020), or mark a sub-era "founder maturation → reflective operator."

**Recommendation:**
- Keep E1 at 2014-2017 but document the sub-era fault line at January 2016 (V2 Games launch) as a sharp tone change.
- Flag 2017-09 through 2017-12 (5 posts) as a *transitional wedge* that could, on closer inspection of E2, belong there instead.
- Does 2017 belong with E1 or E2? On balance: **with E1** because it's still a founder-operational voice and V2 Games is still running. The ML/AI hot-take in Dec 2017 is a seed, not yet a reorientation.
- Does E1 really start in 2014? Yes — but the September 2014 post is essentially a trace signal. Real signal begins Jan 2015. Consider labeling 2014 Q4 as "pre-signal."
