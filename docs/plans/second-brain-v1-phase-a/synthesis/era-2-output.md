## 5. Intra-era contradictions or evolutions

- **Blockchain-bull → Blockchain-substance critic (within weeks, 2018 Jan–Mar):** On 2018-01-19 he cites "experts predict bitcoin to hit $100K by the end of 2018" enthusiastically (urn:li:activity:6360108659015540736). By 2018-02-15 he writes "Somewhere in between blockchain and ICO the product is getting lost" (urn:li:activity:6369983938248699904) and by 2018-03-06 "Blockchain is a database innovation first" (urn:li:activity:6376849689375019008). Evolution: from price-excited to product-grounded inside 45 days. Interpretation: the `pm-taste` lens supersedes the `hype` lens early — this is when his contrarian-structural voice is forged.
- **Entrepreneur-voice → Corporate-learner voice (2018-07-12):** His post announcing the entrepreneurship-to-corporate transition (urn:li:activity:6423036084040630272) marks a measurable shift. Pre-pivot posts (Jan–Jun 2018) are hot-takes / hiring / opinions. Post-pivot posts (Jul 2018 onwards) add reflection, meta-observation, "retrospection" as a keyword. "X" (I am CEO/founder issuing strong opinions) vs "not-X" (I am absorbing a new industry and writing to learn).
- **"Idea is enough" → "You must ship prototype":** 2018–early 2020 posts repeatedly celebrate ideas, validation, STP frameworks. 2020-05-19 (urn:li:activity:6668609461650784256) marks an inversion — he explicitly rejects his own "just an idea!" stance and commits to shipping prototypes via Flutter. "X" (validate the idea through conversation) vs "not-X" (validate the idea by building the prototype yourself). This inversion persists into every future era.
- **Sales-centric vocabulary → Product-centric vocabulary:** Early 2019 posts (Feb–Mar) lean heavily on sales/BD/STP/MMP language. Late 2019 / 2020 posts shift to product/MVP/validation/users. Evolution tracks the Aroma Magic (BD role) → freelance/PM (product role) career move.
- **"Aphorism-dense" → "Framework-dense":** Oct–Nov 2019 is peak one-liner season (many 1–2 sentence posts). By mid-2020 the posts have regained structure — numbered lists, three-pillars, case studies. Interpretation: the aphorism style was a low-bandwidth corporate-BD-job workaround; the framework style returns when he has more project bandwidth (freelance).

## 7. Era boundary notes

The locked era list calls E2 "2018-2020 V2 era" but the data suggests this label is wrong — V2 Games is only the first 5 months of 2018. E2 is actually 3 overlapping mini-eras:

- **E2a (Jan 2018 – May 2018): V2 Games tail / Flow.live blockchain pivot.** ~22 posts, almost entirely blockchain/ICO/crypto/hiring. High opinion density. Very distinct voice from the rest of E2.
- **E2b (Jul 2018 – Dec 2019): Aroma Magic / Blossom Kochhar corporate-BD era.** ~35 posts. Business development, STP, MVP, franchise economics, sales-vs-BD playbook. Aphorism-dense late 2019. This is the "corporate new-industry-learner" register. Nothing blockchain. Nothing product-PM yet.
- **E2c (Jan 2020 – Dec 2020): Freelance / pandemic / job-hunt / learning-in-public era.** ~36 posts. Flutter/Dart acquisition, Python OOP brush-up, Gartner BI analysis, Toggl/mindfulness, free-consulting offer, #OpenToWork, 3-pillars idea validation. The "I am becoming a PM" rehearsal. Ends with FarEye offer Dec 2020.

**Recommended boundary refinement:**

- Either split E2 into E2a/E2b/E2c (above), or
- Rename E2 to "2018–2020 Transition Era" (more accurate than "V2 era"), and
- Move the **FarEye** start boundary forward to Dec 2020 (currently unclear where E3 begins).
