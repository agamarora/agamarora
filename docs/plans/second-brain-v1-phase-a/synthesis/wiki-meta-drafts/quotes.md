# Operator aphorisms. The B2B AI PM working set.

One-liners from twelve years of B2B product practice, organized by domain. Each line is verbatim. Drop any of them in a room.

---

## The agent-first manifesto

> "Start thinking agent first. Not just from a building lens but from a serving lens. That will be the differentiation." *(2025-06-20)*

> "We need to kill prompting. Most of you are wasting your time getting prompt frameworks." *(2024-12-24)*

> "Context > Prompt. Spec > Sprint. Taste > Execution." *(2026-04-09)*

> "The model wasn't broken, the agent layer was." *(2025-11-17)*

> "0% automation driven through UI, 100% driven through MCP and APIs." *(2025-09-29)*

---

## Enterprise AI reality

> "Roughly 80% of enterprise AI experiments don't reach production. The gap between demo and deployment is wider than vendors admit." *(2024-09-04)*

> "Building for a demo vs. building for production is a different beast." *(2024-09-04)*

> "The three durable enterprise AI moats are infrastructure, talent, and data. The model is not on the list." *(2024-12-04)*

> "Almost everyone believes GenAI is overhyped. Yet there is something magically different from other techs that were hyped and never lived up." *(2024)*

> "Smaller implementations are sought after. Time to value is king." *(2024-09-04)*

> "Plug and play is the dream. No one wants to reinvent every time." *(2025-06-20)*

> "I would catch on to the hype once Devin replaces its founders to build Devin 2.0." *(2024)*

## PM as risk mitigation

> "PM is the grind of answering 99 should-we to get to that 1 can-we." *(2022-06-03)*

> "Product management is a featherless hat: omnipresent yet indistinguishable if done properly." *(2021-09-22)*

> "Three in-person user interviews are better than 32 filled surveys. Change my mind." *(2024-03-27)*

> "People are not paying for features. They are paying to solve their problems." *(2024-03-26)*

> "The real threat is not that someone will copy you, but that no one is copying you." *(2024-04-19)*

> "Building a product is like raising a child. Make tough decisions, protect from distortion." *(2021-07-12)*

> "I have a very strong opinion about having no strong opinions." *(2021-12-12)*

> "I am extremely opinionated about adding customization to products. To the point where I feel like I have lost a hard-fought battle with myself if a PRD ends up including customization settings." *(2025-12-04)*

## Career economics

> "Strive to learn concepts, not tools. Be a creator." *(2019-12-04)*

> "The world doesn't need more drivers. AI will make you redundant if not now but soon." *(2019-12-04)*

> "People management is a critical skill but it is not for everyone. Individuals who believe in pursuit of functional expertise can now have a home." *(2021-10-30)*

> "Growth as a product manager is moving from best product to profitable and valuable business." *(2025-11-25)*

> "Never be the smartest person in the room. If you feel you are, get more people in the room or change the room." *(2019-07-11)*

> "Go increase the size of the pie if you want a bigger share." *(2018-07-12)*

> "Value over revenue. Humans over resources. Trust over contracts." *(2019-02-19)*

> "Certifications are collectibles that fuel our personal sense of achievement. They are in no shape or means a validation of what you can and cannot do." *(2024-11-15)*

> "Contribute within the top 20% every 60 days. It is like a privilege bank account with ever-increasing average monthly balance quotas." *(2024-02-11)*

> "The lowest common denominator came out to be hours put in." *(2024-06-25)*
