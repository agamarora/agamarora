## 1. Refined statement

**Canonical (master-list):**
> "All of us know the power of a personal context layer. Every future AI session reads the same brain and writes back to it." External cognitive extension as personal context infrastructure.

**Refined statement (post-R4 evidence pass):**

The second-brain is the context-layer that makes context > prompt operational; an external, git-tracked, AI-readable extension of self that compounds across sessions.
