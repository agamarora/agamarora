## 1. Refined statement (Agam's framing)

**Public canonical, 2022-05-17:**
> "Just because you open an umbrella when it's raining does not mean it will rain whenever you open the umbrella. Not everything can be explained by your data. Identify what can be, and build accordingly."

**What Agam is actually claiming (decomposed):**

1. **Correlation is not causation, and PMs need to know which is which.** The umbrella inversion is a 14-word causal-inference primer: a PM who reads the dashboard without ranking dependency directionality is doing astrology with bigger numbers.
2. **Data has a scope, and the scope must be drawn explicitly.** "Identify what can be, and build accordingly" — bound the data's reach, don't defer to it.
3. **Measurement IS the PM job, not a function-handoff.** The 5 R3e items use first-person plural imperatives ("we measure", "you can keep on hardening"). Data work is the PM's own apparatus, not a data-analyst report.
4. **The PM who can't be debugged from data isn't doing their job.** Frame inversion — PM as debugged-object, not just debugger. Surfaces in the bottleneck post's "fresh eyes on the dashboards" and in the self-instrumentation register. The PM is debuggable because the PM debugged themselves first.
5. **Data literacy spans 5 competencies.** The 5 R3e Collab items map: severity-quantification (bug-triage), base measurement layer (AARRR), continuous-loop discipline (bottleneck), data governance (PII), and data-as-leverage-ladder (4-ways). The cluster IS the belief — no single item carries it.

**Posture/voice notes:** umbrella post = aphorism-register (paradox-as-rhetorical-device, plain-hyphen rhythm, no em-dashes). The 5 Collab items = register-4 structured-prompt-response (opener-thesis → numbered list → close). **Both registers carry this belief** — rare, since most beliefs concentrate in one register. Cross-register coverage signals load-bearing weight in PM identity.

**Frame movement (master-list flagged):** track shift from "PMs should know data" (2017-22) to "PM IS data" (2024-26). Section 4 traces the inflection via self-instrumentation lineage.
