## 1. Refined statement

**Canonical (2024-04-12):** "If your breadth has no depth, you are what one could call a Human-GPT. Choose your niche and invest."

**Refined synthesis:** Breadth of knowledge across domains is a professional differentiator (parent thesis, `belief.breadth-as-differentiation`) — but post-ChatGPT, breadth alone has been commoditized. A generalist with no specialty is now indistinguishable from a chat interface to a foundation model. The remedy is the T-shape: keep the breadth, add a depth-axis. The depth-axis can be vertical (industry, domain) OR horizontal (function, specialization) — niche-choice is a free variable, but choosing one and investing is not. Pivots are permitted; standing-still in pure breadth is not. Late-2024 onward, the depth-axis is reframed: "depth" extends beyond technical expertise to include humanness — connection, EQ, first-principles thinking — the qualities AI cannot simulate. Breadth keeps you efficient; depth makes you loved.

This is a refinement of `belief.breadth-as-differentiation`, not a contradiction. Breadth still differentiates. Depth is now table stakes underneath the breadth.
