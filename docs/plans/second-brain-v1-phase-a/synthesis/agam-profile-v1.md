## How I think

The root is substance over hype. Reduce the category, then evaluate. Below the root, four beliefs do most of the load-bearing work.

**Agent-first.** Platforms that cannot talk to autonomous agents are already behind. The serving lens matters as much as the building lens: your APIs, your data surfaces, your system design must be legible to agents calling on behalf of users, not just to the users themselves. The June 2025 manifesto named this explicitly. By April 2026 it had become flat assumption: the second-brain, the MCP-first voice platform, the spec-first builder posture are all agent-first applied at different layers.

**Spec before sprint.** Spec > Sprint. Taste > Execution. Context > Prompt. Three lines from April 9, 2026, which is new vocabulary for a disposition that has been building since at least 2021. Pre-LLM, the binding constraint was existence: ship the thing or the idea stays theoretical. Post-LLM, iteration is near-zero-cost. The new constraint is quality-of-spec, because AI amplifies whatever you give it. An under-spec'd prompt returns noise at speed. A well-spec'd one returns your vision back, faster. The constraint moved upstream; the behavior adapted.

**Breadth needs depth.** Breadth of knowledge across domains is a genuine differentiator. It speeds pattern recognition, builds cross-team empathy, creates connective tissue specialists miss. But post-ChatGPT, that sentence needs a binding constraint: "If your breadth has no depth, you are what one could call a Human-GPT." Wide, available, useful to a point, replicable by a chat interface. The prescription: choose at least one niche, invest in it, pivot quickly if it's wrong but not slowly.

**PM taste.** Product management is 99% "should we?" to get to the 1% "can we?" That 1% is disruption. The rest is food. The grind of the role is discrimination at scale: knowing what to refuse before a PRD is written, reading data without mistaking correlation for causation, specifying non-functional requirements as primary work not compliance theater. Anti-customization is the personal application of this belief: each setting in a PRD is a design decision deferred to the user, and a pre-paid mortgage on every future implementation.

## How I write

Four modes, each for a specific surface and register.

The take: bold declarative opener, structured body, zinger close or a heart. "We need to kill prompting." "The real threat is not that someone will copy you, but that no one is copying you." Lead with the thesis, no warm-up, no conclusion recap.

The four-word reply: "Quit quitting." "It's an enhancement not a bug." Comment mode. Compressed. Sometimes in Hindi when the person is a friend.

The playbook: when someone asks a specific how-do-I question, the full stack without gatekeeping. No positioning in mentor mode. Just teach.

The framework drop: opener-thesis, three to five items in `1/ ` slash-format, optional aphorism close. This is how 58 LinkedIn Collaborative Article responses earned Top Voice in PM and AI (top 1-2% globally). Dense. Practitioner-voice.

What I do not do: hashtags (zero since 2023), em-dashes (statistically absent from 287 posts), "In today's fast-paced world" openers, generic motivational sign-offs, conclusion-recap paragraphs.

The colon is my rhythm tool. 1.24 per post in the current era, highest frequency punctuation. "Title: explanation" is the crystallized sentence shape. The heart closer is for personal and reflective posts that earned it. The PS is for the thing I almost didn't say.

## What I am still figuring out

Seven questions from three years of public thinking that the corpus does not close:

1. **The kill-prompting arc.** "We need to kill prompting" (2024-12-24) was an overstated verb for a correct diagnosis. By July 2025, prompts were re-admitted as engineering plumbing. The arc is public. Whether to name the U-turn explicitly on the wiki as an honest waypoint, or treat it as a refined-and-moved-on arc without the overshoot labeled: this is a voice call, not a fact call.

2. **Application-layer AI-PM boundary in 2026.** The 2023 line "ChatGPT for everything except product management" is now historical: by 2026, Claude Code is inside PM craft. The boundary between "I build applications on models" and "I use models to build the spec itself" has blurred. The working frame - application-layer vs foundation-layer - still holds as an identity claim. Whether it holds as a precise technical description is less certain.

3. **Second-brain generalizability.** The honest scoping from the launch post: "Mine is more of a personal framework that is working for me for past 4 months. This post is mostly for hobbyist like me who want a barebones, Karpathy inspired, wiki version." The belief statement has no such hedge: "All of us know the power of a personal context layer." Which is the right frame at 2026-04-25?

4. **Humanness as depth axis.** April 2024 prescription led with EQ. September 2024 Mo Gawdat citation confirmed the humanness-as-depth frame. Whether this is Agam's own synthesis that Mo Gawdat confirmed, or a borrowed frame found useful but not fully owned, matters for how prominently the wiki presents it.

5. **Anti-customization scope.** The economic argument (six months of implementation, an implementation partner, hundreds of training documents) is enterprise-shaped. Whether the belief holds equally for consumer products - where the customization cost structure is different - or whether the strongest version of it is specifically enterprise-rooted: taste-pass decides.

6. **Strong opinion about no strong opinions.** Approximately 30 strong opinions on record. Also this: "I have a very strong opinion about having no strong opinions" (2021-12-12). The paradox is the voice. Whether to name it explicitly on the wiki as the meta-belief that licenses all the others, or leave it as a reader-detected register: an Agam call.

7. **The tinker-publicly loop.** Does the tinkering feel incomplete until it is posted and someone learns from it? If yes, the build-post-teach pattern is loop-closure. If no, posting is a distribution decision separate from building. The answer changes how the personal-projects-tinkering page frames the builder identity.
