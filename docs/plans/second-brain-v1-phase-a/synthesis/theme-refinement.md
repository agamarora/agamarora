## Section 1: Executive verdict

**Locked §4 proposed 10 themes.** After R4 deep-dive evidence passes on 35 beliefs, R5 proposes **12 final themes** (net +2). The two additions are: (a) `spec-first-taste` split from `pm-taste` — the 2026-04-09 trilogy (Spec > Sprint / Taste > Execution / Context > Prompt) has sufficient evidence density and distinct enough register to stand alone; (b) `personal-projects-tinkering` formalized as a real theme rather than a residual bucket. The locked `voice-ai-craft` theme is **retained with scope refinement**: it remains its own wiki page (per Decision 2's "craft/principle, not identity" framing), but it is explicitly positioned as a satellite page to `enterprise-ai-reality` and `agent-first`, not as a peer-level thesis theme. The locked `humor-wit` theme is **dropped as a standalone wiki page** — R4 evidence confirms it is a voice-register not a belief cluster, and it will be captured in the voice-spec (R8) not the wiki. The net result: 10 → 12 live wiki theme pages, minus 1 dropped (humor-wit), plus 2 added, plus one root page (substance-over-hype) that sits above the theme grid.

**Biggest re-clustering:** (1) `pm-taste` splits into `pm-taste` (craft metaphors, anti-customization, metaphor-stack) and `spec-first-taste` (spec > sprint, taste > execution, context > prompt — the 2026 builder trilogy). These are thematically adjacent but serve different reader journeys: pm-taste is "how does Agam think about what a PM does?" while spec-first-taste is "how does Agam think about what a builder does with AI tools?". (2) `substance-over-hype` is elevated OUT of the peer-theme list and becomes the **wiki root disposition** — the lens through which all other themes are read. It is not Theme 11; it is the epistemological frame that conditions Themes 1-11. (3) `thinking-in-writing` from the master-list intermediate clustering collapses fully into `linkedin-as-instrumental-platform` with an aphorism sub-register — the writing-as-reasoning thread is a behavior pattern, not a theme distinct enough from the platform theme.
